Lotusks mission is to provide you with relatable and meaningful good quality jewellery to remind and encourage you to have faith in embracing your journey and the beautiful person that you become because of it. Choose Love with Lotusk. Lotusk aims to show that we can enjoy beauty products without supporting animal cruelty and unethical processes. Lotusks eyelashes are made from synthetic fibres which are 100% vegan and cruelty-free. So we can enjoy beauty without cruelty.
